Our Journey

A Legacy of Excellence

From a founding vision to a nationwide network — our story is built on bold decisions and trusted partnerships.

2010
Company Founded

Nama United was established in Cairo with a clear vision: to professionalize the distribution of automotive spare parts across Egypt with international quality standards.

2013
Dealer Network Expansion

Reached 50 authorized dealers across major Egyptian governorates, establishing a reliable distribution infrastructure.

2015
First Exclusive Agency — KDD China

Secured exclusive import and distribution rights for KDD, a leading Chinese automotive parts manufacturer known for engine and chassis components.

2018
Borsching Germany Partnership

A landmark agreement with Borsching brought premium European engineering to our catalog — suspension, steering, and braking systems meets OEM standards.

2021
Vika China — Third Exclusive Brand

Added Vika to our portfolio, expanding coverage into body parts, electrical components, and cooling systems with wide vehicle compatibility.

2024
Digital B2B Dealer Portal

Launched our comprehensive digital platform, giving 200+ authorized dealers real-time access to wholesale pricing, inventory, and online invoicing.
