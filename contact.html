FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about partnering with NUTD.

How do I become an authorized dealer?
You can apply through our online Dealer Registration form. Our team reviews all applications and will respond within 2–3 business days with next steps.
What is the minimum order quantity for wholesale?
Minimum order quantities vary by product category. Registered dealers receive competitive wholesale pricing with flexible MOQs. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.
Do you serve customers outside Cairo?
Yes! We have a nationwide dealer network spanning all governorates. Our logistics partners ensure reliable delivery across Egypt.
Are your products covered by warranty?
All NUTD products carry manufacturer warranties. Additionally, we offer our own NUTD Quality Guarantee. You can register and track warranties through our online warranty portal.
How can I check part compatibility for my vehicle?
You can use our online catalog with built-in compatibility filters or contact our technical support team via phone or WhatsApp for expert assistance.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, credit terms for approved dealers, and cash payments at our warehouse. Dealer portal users can also manage invoices and payments directly online.
